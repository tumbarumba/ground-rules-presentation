Mutual Learning Assumptions

Assumption: I have some information; so do other people

Assumption: Each of us may see things that others don't

Assumption: I may be contibuting to the problem

Assumption: Differences may be opportunities for learning

Assumption: People may disagree with me and have pure motives

Ground Rules for Effective Teams

Rule 1: State views and ask genuine questions

Rule 2: Share all relevant information

Rule 3: Use specific examples and agree on what important words mean

Rule 4: Explain reasoning and intent

Rule 5: Focus on interests, not positions

Rule 6: Test assumptions and inferences

Rule 7: Jointly design next steps

Rule 8: Discuss undiscussable issues
